About Us
The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education.
The major objective of the ISTE are:
  • Providing quality training programmes to teachers and administrators of technical institutions to update their knowledge and skills in their fields of activity.
  • To assist and contribute in the production and development of top quality professional engineers and technicians needed by the industry and other organisations.
  • Providing guidance and training to students to develop better learning skills and personality.
What are the advantages of being an ISTE member?
  • A Member of a National Professional Organisation
  • Join an academic fraternity of over 50,000 strong professionals
  • to attend short-term training programmes for academic excellence and to become eligible for career advancement opportunities
  • Eligible to apply for various national level awards
  • Publications for Self Development, Institution Development, etc. (at concessional rates)
  • Eligible to participate in Chapter Level, Section Level and National Level Conferences, Workshops and other activities of your academic interest, etc.
Events
We conduct some of the most renowned and fun events here at ISTE!!
Best Engineer
Best Engineer is a 5 phase event conducted exclusively for students of MBCET. The event is a hunt for the Best Engineer in the campus, in technical, soft skill, marketing and basic engineering aspects.
Techsule
Techsule stands for Technological Capsule. It is an initiative by ISTE MBCET Student's Chapter to update the students about upcoming technologies in the fields of Electronic, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil and Computer Science Engineering.
Memoir
ISTE MBCET Student's Chapter in collaboration with MBCET Alumni Association Middle East, conducted Memoir, a series of 14 episodes. Through Memoir, the alumni shared their experiences and memories regarding MBCET.
